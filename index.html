Return Policy

At Jammy Box, we strive to provide our customers with high-quality products and excellent service.

Due to the nature of our business, we do not accept returns for change-of-mind or discretionary reasons. We encourage our customers to carefully consider their purchases before finalizing them.

We understand that there can be exceptional circumstances, such as receiving a damaged or defective item. In such cases, we will gladly replace the item or issue a Free refund within 30 days of purchase.

Please contact our customer service team at office@jammybox.us to report any issues with your order.

Please note the following exceptions to our return and refund policy:

  • Discounted items are final and cannot be returned or exchanged.
  • Returned items must be returned in original product packaging.
  • Returned items must have no visible signs of use.

Return Process

To initiate a return, please complete the following steps:

  • Contact Us and send an email for a refund/return request with products you would like to return.
  • After you receive a return confirmation email from us, pack your package securely and return to us with shipping label attatched that was within the original package.

    • - When a refund is processed, we will cover the cost of return shipping for damaged returns or exchanges.

  • After we receive and check the item, we will send you a refund confirmation email and proceed a refund/return with in 5-7 business days. Please note that the original shipping fee will not be refunded.

We suggest allowing up to 30 days for a refund to show in your account as credits issued to PayPal may take longer appear. If it’s been more than 30 days, contact us for more help.

h2>Delivery Policy

At Jammy Box, we are committed to providing you with a seamless and efficient delivery experience. Please read our delivery policy carefully to understand how we handle the shipment and delivery of your orders.

Processing Time

Orders are processed and dispatched within 1-3 business days after payment confirmation. However, some orders may be shipped within as few as 4 hours and some may be shipped within 3 full business days. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays. Orders placed on Friday after 12PM PST or over the weekend will begin processing on the following Monday. If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments maybe delayed by a few days.

Shipping Method

We offer shipping only with in 48 contigous United States. Shipping costs and estimated delivery times will be calculated at checkout based on your location and the chosen shipping method.

USPS Priority Mail® $9.35 - $12.55 1-3 Business Days 48 Contigous U.S.

Please Note :

You will receive your order anywhere from 1-7 business days from the date that it is shipped out, not the date the order is placed.

Lost or Stolen Packages

Jammy Box is not responsible for lost or stolen packages. In the event a package is lost or stolen, either the receiver or Jammy Box will need to contact UPS to open an investigation. We are unable to move forward until the investigation is complete on UPS' end. Kindly note, in some cases, UPS may require a police report to open an investigation.

Received Incorrect Item

Please contact us immediately at office@jammybox.us if you received an incorrect item, so we can process your return request as soon as possible. If the correct item is available, we will ship the replacement once the wrong item has been received for return by UPS. If the correct item is not available, refund will be issued once the returned merchandise is received and processed by our returns department.
